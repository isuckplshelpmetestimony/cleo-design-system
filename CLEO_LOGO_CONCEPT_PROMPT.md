# Cleo Logo Concept Generation — AI Prompt

Copy everything below the line into a design AI (Midjourney, Ideogram, Figma AI, Adobe Firefly, etc.) when generating **many logo concepts** for **Cleo**. Run in batches: use the **Concept buckets** section to request 5–10 variations per bucket, then refine winners.

Reference: [Cleo Design System](https://cleo-ds.netlify.app/) · [Color foundations](https://cleo-ds.netlify.app/foundations/color/)

---

## Role

You are a senior brand designer creating **logo and wordmark concepts** for **Cleo** — AI-assisted clinical documentation and denial prevention for **outpatient physical therapy**. Every concept must embody **Quiet Authority**: confidence without shouting, warmth without performance, a mark that **steps back as work completes**.

This is **exploration**, not final brand lock. Generate **high volume and high variety** — then filter ruthlessly against the criteria below.

---

## What Cleo is (context)

- **Product:** Helps licensed PTs finish notes faster, submit cleaner claims, and catch denial risk before signing — without startup hype or alarmist UI.
- **Audience:** Clinic lead or senior PT, 8–15 years in practice. Cognitively exhausted, skeptical of “AI-powered” marketing, burned by denials after doing everything right.
- **Emotional north star (Frame 8):** *Home for dinner on a Tuesday* — sun still up, charting done, walking through the front door. The logo should feel like **arrival and relief**, not hustle or clinical sterility.
- **Design philosophy:** [Quiet Authority](https://cleo-ds.netlify.app/introduction/quiet-authority/) — cool restrained palette, humanist serif authority, **one** warm accent per composition, generous space, plain competence.
- **Current placeholder:** Wordmark “Cleo” in **Lora** serif only; favicon is slate square + cream “C”. **Logo mark is intentionally open** — explore freely but stay on-system.

**The test for every concept:** Would a tired, skeptical therapist trust this mark before they feel marketed to? If it reads as SaaS startup, hospital system, or wellness spa — reject it.

| Competence | Feels like someone who has done this a thousand times |

**Voice alignment (for wordmarks):** Senior clinician colleague. Warm, never casual. No all-caps aggression, no bold 700+ weights, no tight tracking.

## Color system (exact hex only)

Use **only** these colors. No pure white `#FFFFFF`, no pure black `#000000`, no bright startup blue, no neon, no gradients unless explicitly noted as a rejected “don’t” example.

| Token | Hex | Logo use |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Page | `#F5F3F0` | Light backgrounds, mockup canvas |
| Sidebar | `#E2E5EA` | Secondary lockups, doc-style previews |
| Fog | `#F4F5F7` | Subtle fields in composite mockups |
| Midnight | `#0F1F30` | Primary dark ink, dark lockups |
| Slate | `#2E4057` | Default mark color, structure |
| Steel | `#8C95A6` | Secondary lines, muted variants |
| Cream | `#F5EBD6` | Text on slate/midnight backgrounds |
| Amber | `#D4841A` | **At most one warm accent per lockup** — lamp, dot, check, underline. Never compete with a second warm color |
| Green | `#3D6B4F` | Rare; success only if concept demands it — prefer slate/amber |
| Red | `#8B3A3A` | Avoid in logo — alarm association |

**Single-accent rule (critical):** Amber is the **only** warm accent in the entire Cleo system — mirroring “one moment of warmth” (dinner light, completion check). In a logo: **one** amber element maximum (e.g. dot on a letter, small check, horizon sun). Never amber + orange + gold together.

**Preferred lockups:**
- Primary: **Slate or midnight** mark on `#F5F3F0` page
- Reversed: **Cream** mark on `#0F1F30` or `#2E4057`
- Favicon/app icon: simplified mark; readable at **16–32px**

## Typography alignment (wordmarks)

Ideal headline face: **Freight Display** (reference). Implementation: **Lora** (Google Fonts) — humanist serif, bracketed serifs, moderate contrast, wide unhurried letterforms.

**Wordmark rules:**
- Weight **400 only** (no bold, no 700+)
- Title case **“Cleo”** — not CLEO, not cleo
- Letter-spacing slightly tight on hero scale only (`-0.01em` max) — never fashionable tight tracking
- Pairing: mark + wordmark; wordmark alone is valid

**Do not evoke:** Inter, Roboto, geometric sans logos, all-caps tech wordmarks, Didot fashion serifs, script/handwriting “friendly AI” fonts.

---

## Visual language (from illustration & UI)

Cleo illustration register (Lim Heng Swee–inspired): **flat color planes**, hard clean edges, **organic forms**, generous negative space, max **5 colors** from palette, **one** amber warm element in a cool field.

**Subject matter that fits:** journey, openness, doorway, horizon, table/light, path, listening stillness, completion, stepping back.

**Strictly prohibited in logo concepts:**
- Medical iconography: crosses, caduceus, stethoscopes, anatomy, spines, bones, hands-on-hip clinicians
- PT clichés: dumbbells, treadmills, exercise bands as hero motifs
- “AI” tropes: brains, sparkles, robots, circuit nodes, chat bubbles, magic wands
- SaaS tropes: upward arrows, rocket ships, infinite loops, hexagons, gradient orbs
- Stock “healthtech”: bright teal + purple gradients, glowing nodes, shield-with-checkmark
- Alarm: exclamation marks, warning triangles, aggressive red
- Loud motion: speed lines, swooshes, bounce shapes
- Geometric abstract art: random faceted polygons, meaningless monoline knots
- Gradients, textures, drop shadows, 3D gloss, metallic effects
- Competitor energy: flooded multicolor, multiple warm accents, bright blue primary

## Concept buckets (generate many — vary widely)

For each bucket, produce **5–10 distinct directions**. Mix **symbol-only**, **wordmark-only**, and **combination** marks.

### A — Quiet Authority (typographic)
- Lora wordmark refinements: ligatures, terminal shapes, subtle serif brackets
- Monogram **C** with humanist proportions (not a generic circle badge)
- Hairline **0.5px** slate rules as part of lockup (editorial, not underline gimmick)

### B — Single warm accent (amber discipline)
- One amber dot: period, tittle on **i**, sun on horizon, lamp, plate rim — **only one**
- Amber completion check integrated subtly (small, not a green SaaS checkmark)
- Compare: same mark **without** amber vs **with** one amber element — amber version should feel like Frame 8

### C — Arrival & home (Frame 8)
- Doorway / threshold / path leading to light
- Table + window + low sun (abstract flat planes, not literal dining illustration)
- Horizon line: cool slate sky, single amber sun
- “Walking home” as negative space path — minimal, not figurative people

### D — Stepping back / completion
- Mark that **opens up** or **recedes** as if UI quieting after sign
- Enclosure that releases (frame opening, bracket relaxing, line resolving)
- Empty space as intentional — “room to breathe” after work is done

### E — Listening & documentation (abstract, restrained)
- Single calm **waveform** stroke (slate, 1–2 lines max) — not “voice assistant” mic
- Page / note silhouette as negative space — not a clipboard cartoon
- Line that becomes check — documentation → done (one stroke story, very simple)

### F — Organic symbol (non-geometric)
- Soft pebble, leaf, river bend, arch — **organic**, hard color edges between planes
- Avoid symmetry-for-symmetry’s sake; slight asymmetry feels human

### G — Letterform integration
- **C** as enclosure, crescent, or partial circle — moon/clear night subtlety (not literal moon cliché unless exceptional)
- **eo** ligature or connected letterforms suggesting flow or continuity
- Lowercase exploration **only if** it still reads authoritative (default is “Cleo” title case)

### H — App icon / favicon
- Must read at **16px**: 1–2 shapes max, no thin hairlines
- Square with **8px** corner radius logic (match UI `--radius-sm`)
- Versions: slate on page, cream on midnight

### I — Reversed & environmental
- Cream on midnight hero band (marketing dark sections)
- Sidebar preview: mark at ~24px beside “Cleo” in Lora at 20px on `#E2E5EA`

### J — Explicit anti-references (generate to discard)
- Produce 2–3 **intentionally wrong** concepts labeled “rejected” (startup blue, medical cross, AI sparkle) to calibrate the model — do not refine these.

## Composition & craft rules

- **Simplicity:** Logo must survive favicon, sidebar (~24px), and letterhead. Prefer 1–3 elements.
- **Weight:** Confidence from **proportion and space**, not thick strokes or bold type.
- **Contrast:** Midnight/slate on page — never pure black on white.
- **Space:** Generous clear space; mark should feel like **putting something down**, not picking something up.
- **Edges:** Clean, hard divisions between color planes (illustration register) — no fuzzy gradients.
- **Motion:** Static marks only — no implied bounce, swirl, or “launch” energy.
- **Gender / figure:** No literal human illustrations in the logo.

2. **Name** (2–4 words, e.g. “Horizon Check”, “Open C”)

Generate **minimum 40 concepts** before narrowing. Favor **breadth** over polish in round one.
